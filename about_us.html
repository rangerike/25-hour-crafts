About Us

25th Hour Crafts is the brainchild of Kayla and Scott Bitz brought to life by our imaginations and talents. And nerdiness. You can't forget nerdiness.

Located in Oak Creek, WI, we travel around the upper midwest appearing in the Artist Alley at conventions like Chicago Tardis, CONsole Room, Concinnity/Kappa Kon, and Wizard World in Madison, WI and if you have any events or conventions you would like to see us at give us a shout out at craftyhobbitshop@gmail.com.

Kayla's Crafts

Kayla is, by far, the craftier of the two and makes most of the items we offer like:

  • Dangle Earrings from beads and/or charms
  • Charm necklaces
  • Knitted scarves of both the Fashion and Nerdy variety like the 4th Doctor's Scarf
  • Crocheted Amigurumi and Plushies
  • Licensed Fleece single layer blankets with a chrocheted edge

She also chrochets blankets, shawls, and pillows and knits shawls for friends, family, and volunteer work like Threads of Compassion.

Scott's Crafts

Scott pretty much only does chainmaille like making bags, bracelets, earrings, and dragons. He also helps with the earrings and necklaces, but that's mostly just for putting on the earwires and clasps since he knows how to close jump rings.

Together Crafts

Since they're a married couple they do some things together like making the Licensed cotton fabric bags and setting up their booth together, but that's not really a craft. Scott handles the cutting, pinning, flipping, and drawstring threading and Kayla does most, if not all, of the sewing.
