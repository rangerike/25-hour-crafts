Nerdy & Geeky Crafts

Whether you're looking for a piece of jewelry to subtley show off your nerdy side, a bag to hold all those gaming dice, or a gift for that lovable geek in your life, 25th Hour Crafts has something handmade just for you!

We make all kinds of items from knitted scarves like the 4th Dr's Scarf from Dr. Who to chainmaille dicebags.

Kayla also chrochets amigurumi and blankets, makes charm necklaces and earrings that dangle, and sews whereas Scott handles all the chainmaille items like the aforementioned dicebags, bracelets or varying weaves and colors, and earrings.

Many of these items we keep in stock for conventions around the upper midwest so you can check out our events page for where we'll be next, but we can also make something just for you and send it right to your door.
