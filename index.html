Nerdy - Elegant - Practical

Whether you're looking for something to subtley show off your nerdy side, a bag to hold all those RPG dice, or a gift for that lovable geek in your life, 25th Hour Crafts has something handmade just for you!

We have a slew of premade items that we take to conventions around the upper upper midwest, but we can also make something custom just for you to fit your specific nerd or geek.

25th Hour Crafts is the brainchild of Kayla and Scott Bitz brought to life by our imaginations and talents. Oh, and nerdiness. You can't forget the nerdiness.

So far, we've travelled around the upper midwest appearing as artists at conventions like Chicago Tardis, CONsole Room, Concinnity/Kappa Kon, and our next one will be Wizard World in Madison, WI where we'll hopefully get to see Stan Lee!

We make all sorts of things like:

  • Drawstring and Zipper Dicebags from nerdy fabric
  • Earrings from beads, charms, and chainmaille
  • Chainmaille bracelets
  • Nerdy charm necklaces
  • Chainmaille dicebags
  • Knitted scarves of both the Fashion and Nerdy variety like the 4th Doctor's Scarf
  • Crocheted, Nerdy Amigurumi

And that's not an exhaustive list of all the different things we can make!
